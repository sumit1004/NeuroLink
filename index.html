About NeuroLink

NeuroLink is envisioned as an AI/AR/VR-driven web app to serve as a personal digital caregiver: it continuously learns the user’s history and preferences (from photos, notes, routines) and uses voice, vision and intelligent alerts to remind, reassure and engage them. Inspired by recent advances in memory care AI companions

NeuroLink’s goal is to help patients recall people, places and events, maintain routines and stay safe — while keeping family in the loop with updates..
